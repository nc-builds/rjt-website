About

Most people hear a trombone and think jazz club.
He took his into arenas.

Rahul Joshua Thomas, better known as RJT, is one of India's rare contemporary trombonists. A multi-instrumentalist who plays six instruments, DJs, produces, and has collaborated with over 50 artists across the country and around the world. Touring since 2014, with over 1,500 live shows and counting.

He's the brass behind Anirudh Ravichander's Hukum World Tour, filling arenas from London's O2 to Sydney's Qudos Bank. He's who Benny Dayal trusts on stage night after night. He co-founded Clown With A Frown (India's only pop/funk brass outfit) and SaxBox, because why not mix beatbox with horns.

From the Commonwealth Games in Australia to Lollapalooza India, from Coke Studio sessions to the Bombay Chamber Orchestra, RJT moves between worlds with the kind of fluency that only comes from genuine musical depth.

The Journey

Schooled in brass,
forged on tour

Drag to explore
2004
Frank Anthony Public School Brass Band
Where it started. Classical training under Mr. V. Narayanaswamy. You learn to read before you learn to play.
2011
India National Youth Orchestra
2nd Trombonist. First time playing outside India. Orchestras teach you things a jam session never will.
2013
Founded Clown With A Frown
India's only pop/funk brass outfit. Nobody was doing it, so they invented it.
2014
Touring Begins
The year the road became home. Averaging 120+ shows a year ever since.
2015
Madboy/Mink & The Ska Vengers
Electro-cabaret one night, ska the next. If you can play both, people remember.
2017
Benny Dayal & Funktuation
The long-running partnership begins. Toured everywhere. Try hearing Funktuation without the horns. You can't.
2018
Commonwealth Games, Gold Coast
Opening ceremony with The Ska Vengers. SaxBox co-founded. Coke Studio Tamil.
2022
7-City US Tour
Kansas City to New York. Chicago to Los Angeles. Seattle to Portland. The American chapter.
2024
Hukum World Tour
Anirudh Ravichander. London O2. Sydney Qudos Bank. Zurich Hallenstadion. The biggest rooms he'd ever played.
Now
Still Moving
Point Blank School, London. Teaching brass. 1,500+ shows deep and still learning what the instrument can do.
